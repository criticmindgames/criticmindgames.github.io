## Privacy Policy

At [criticmind.games](https://criticmind.games), we are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of privacy for our users. We do not collect, retain, or process any personal data through our games or website. Our services are structured so that you can enjoy them without providing any personal information.

We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies that would identify or profile our users. No personal identifiers, such as names, email addresses, or device information, are requested or stored at any point during your interaction with our services.

Should you have any questions or require clarification regarding our privacy policy, please contact us at <support@criticmind.games>. We appreciate your trust and thank you for choosing our games.
