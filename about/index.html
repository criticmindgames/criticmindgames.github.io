    • About

    Criticmind Games is the dedicated game development division of Criticmind Inc, focused on creating casual, logic-driven, and mind-sharpening experiences.
    We believe that games can be both entertaining and enriching, offering players moments of fun while stimulating creativity, memory, and problem-solving skills.

    Our team explores simple, elegant mechanics that are easy to learn but rewarding to master. Whether it’s a quick puzzle, a strategic challenge, or a relaxing brain teaser, each project is crafted with care and attention to detail.

    This website serves as a living gallery of what we build.
    At criticmind.games, you’ll find:

    • Mini-games designed for short, engaging play sessions
    • Logic puzzles and brain challenges for players who enjoy thinking outside the box
    • Experimental prototypes, where we test new ideas, mechanics, and interactive concepts
    • Early releases that evolve over time as we refine and improve them

    Criticmind Games is inspired by the joy of discovery — the feeling of solving a tricky puzzle or unlocking a new insight.
    Our goal is to bring that feeling to players around the world, one small game at a time.

    Thank you for exploring our universe of logic and creativity.
    We hope our games become a part of your daily inspiration, offering moments of clarity, challenge, and play.
